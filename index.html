In terms of professional experience, I obtained a practical-oriented bachelor's degree that equipped me with diverse skills, enabling me to create journalistic content for print, radio, and television. During my bachelor's program, I completed two separate two-month internships in distinct sectors. My initial internship took place in the web department at Roularta Media Group in Brussels.

Within the editorial team, my responsibilities included article writing, utilizing Wordpress, and translating content from the magazine's Flemish counterpart. Subsequently, I embarked on a second internship with the French sports media outlet, BmxMedia. During this experience, my primary task was to produce a documentary in Brittany, narrating the story of Mathéo Briantais, encompassing his passion for BMX and the repercussions of his accident. This documentary provided a comprehensive learning experience, involving every aspect of the project, from preparation and filming to post-production.

Currently, I am concurrently employed by two companies while pursuing my studies. Firstly, with "My Sherpa", I offer private lessons in various subjects such as English, French, and study methodology. Secondly, I work with "Xtra Via", an interim company collaborating directly with the prestigious caterer "Choux de Bruxelles," specializing in luxury events. Over the past two years, my involvement has granted me insights into the intricacies of the events industry, contributing significantly to my development in terms of teamwork and stress management.

In addition to my professional experiences, I played the role of a journalist during the 2024 edition of the JPJ (Jeunesse et Parlement des Jeunes) parliamentary simulation. This unique event provided an opportunity to engage in dynamic debates on various bills alongside young speakers from Belgium, including Dutch, French, and German participants.

As a JPJ journalist, I closely monitored parliamentary proceedings, reported on committee debates, and contributed to the creation of daily newspapers and evening news bulletins. This experience not only provided insights into the Belgian political system but also enhanced my ability to find compromises and build coalitions, aligning seamlessly with my broader skill set and professional development.
